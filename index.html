About Chef Michelle

Where Food Is Celebration

My love for cooking started in Atlanta, where I was exposed to diverse cuisines — Italian, Mediterranean, Russian, German, and Indian. Sharing a meal is one of the most intimate ways to connect, which led me to become a personal chef.

I focus on using quality, non-GMO ingredients, believing food is both medicine and a source of joy. Being a personal chef is a dream come true, and I strive to make every event healthy, pleasurable, and full of love.
